Emily Brick is currently a Senior Product Designer at BuzzFeed

...where she focuses on the site experience and CSS styleguide. She’s worked on the BuzzFeed homepage redesign, BuzzFeed Tasty, and the team’s Content Management System.

Before joining BuzzFeed, Emily was a product designer for the 3d Printing Marketplace, Shapeways. Prior to that, she freelanced for Birchbox and interned for NBC Sports.

When she’s not working, she’s probably talking about her dog on Twitter. She spends a decent amount of time writing light front-end code for both work and play. If you asked her one thing she really cares about, it’d probably be the Earth.
