Emily Brick is currently a Product Designer for Trello in New York City

Prior to joining Trello, she focused on the site experience and design systems as a Senior Product Designer for BuzzFeed. She’s worked on the BuzzFeed homepage redesign, BuzzFeed Tasty, and the team’s Content Management System. Before BuzzFeed, Emily was a product designer for the 3D Printing Marketplace, Shapeways.
